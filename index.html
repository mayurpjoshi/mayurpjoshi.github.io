About Me

I am an Assistant Professor specializing in information systems, digital transformation, and organizational theory. My research focuses on understanding how artificial intelligence and digital technologies transform organizations, work practices, and decision-making processes.

With publications in top-tier journals such as the Journal of Management Studies and JAIS, and recognition through multiple editorial awards, I am committed to advancing our understanding of the socio-technical aspects of digital transformation. My work bridges theory and practice, contributing to both academic discourse and real-world applications.

Artificial Intelligence

Digital Transformation

Understanding how organizations navigate digital transformation, develop capabilities, and adapt to technological change. I investigate the strategic, operational, and cultural dimensions of digitalization in contemporary organizations.

Organization Theory
